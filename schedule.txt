MAG7_GEX — SCHEDULE
===================

Cron (5-field, LOCAL time):   0 8 * * 1-5
Meaning:                      8:00 AM, Monday through Friday
Frequency:                    once per trading day

Target: 10:00 AM ET (~30 min after the 9:30 ET open).
Cron runs in the machine's LOCAL timezone, so set the hour to hit 10am ET:

  Timezone        Cron            Local time
  --------        ----            ----------
  Eastern  (ET)   0 10 * * 1-5    10:00 AM
  Central  (CT)   0 9  * * 1-5     9:00 AM
  Mountain (MT)   0 8  * * 1-5     8:00 AM   <-- current setting
  Pacific  (PT)   0 7  * * 1-5     7:00 AM

Note: the app applies a small deterministic dispatch delay (a few minutes),
so runs may fire a couple minutes after the top of the hour.

Notes:
- Runs only while the Claude app is open; if closed at run time, fires on next launch.
- Weekends are skipped by the cron (1-5 = Mon-Fri); market holidays are skipped by
  the task's own trading-day check.
